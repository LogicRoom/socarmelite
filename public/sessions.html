Carmelite Sessions is a curated live-recording series designed to bridge the gap between the studio and the dancefloor. After 12 months of production and 30+ unreleased tracks, Session 001 moves the project into a live environment. We are bringing together a hand-picked group of individuals to capture the energy of these sets for our upcoming social media series. This is an invitation to be part of the process.

The Collective.

"Real connection requires a physical space and a shared frequency."

Carmelite Sessions was born from a realization: modern clubbing is broken. You can go to a top-tier venue in London, but it is almost always too noisy and chaotic to actually build a meaningful connection. Digital success and remote work mean nothing without a real-world community, so we are building an invite-only collective with actual standards.

We are creating a space that naturally fosters fun, effortless conversation. We use premium house music and the energy of the dancefloor as the ultimate icebreaker, allowing you to step away from the screens and meet genuinely cool, driven people.

Our acid test for every event? It must be completely immersive, engaging, and enjoyable even if you don't drink. This isn't just a one-off showcase; it is a highly curated environment designed for you to find real value and actually look forward to getting out of the house again.
