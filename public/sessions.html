CARMELITE. SESSIONS 001

Private Studio Showcase, London.

Saturday 21st March | 5pm-9pm, Camden

Carmelite Sessions is a curated live-recording series designed to bridge the gap between the studio and the dancefloor. After 12 months of production and 30+ unreleased tracks, Session 001 moves the project into a live environment. We are bringing together a hand-picked group of individuals to capture the energy of these sets for our upcoming social media series. This is an invitation to be part of the process.

The Venue.

Located in the heart of Camden we have chosen a venue which has everything to create a comfortable, creative fun space to dance, connect with everyone at the event and be part of some high quality recorded content which you can take with you! There will be a champagne reception and drinks provided when you arrive!

The Artists.

We have curated a lineup of premier London talent. Supporting Carmelite (Pete) in the debut event is Enrico and Exotico (AJ).

Enrico Chirchiello

A veteran of the underground, Enrico’s career spans residencies at Ministry of Sound and Pacha. Sound rooted in old-school house with Latin rhythms.

Carmelite (Pete)

The creative force behind the sessions, Carmelite is a deep tech house producer. Session 001 serves as the live debut for over 30 unreleased tracks.

Exotico

Born and bred in London with deep Colombian roots, Exotico blends his heritage with the grit of the UK rave scene. Unique Latin-tech and dark house.
